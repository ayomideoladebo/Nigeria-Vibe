SERVER RULES

Welcome to Horizon RP. To ensure a fair, immersive, and high-quality experience for everyone, all citizens are required to adhere to the following legislation. Ignorance is no excuse.

Roleplay & Immersion

  • Stay In-Character: No mixing OOC/IC chat during active RP. Use '/b' for non-RP talk.
  • Powergaming (PG): Do not force unpreventable actions on others without giving them a chance to respond.
  • Metagaming (MG): Do not use information gained outside of roleplay (Discord, streams, etc.) to influence IC actions.
  • Fail RP: Act realistically. Drive, speak, and react as a real person would.
  • Fear RP: Value your character's life. Do not act recklessly when facing deadly threats (guns, cops).
  • Name Policies: Character names must be realistic formats (First_Last). No celebrities or memes.

Crime & Violence

  • No RDM (Random Deathmatch): Attacking another player must be preceded by high-quality roleplay and a valid motive.
  • No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch): Do not use your vehicle as a weapon to ram or kill outside of heavily escalated RP.
  • High-Tier Crime: Extreme violence, kidnappings, or hits require significant planning and a deep IC reason. Low-effort robbery spam is forbidden.
  • Gang Initiation: Turf claiming and gang formation must be approved by management.

Communication

  • Language: English is the primary language for all public IC communications.
  • Zero Tolerance Harassment: Hate speech, racism, extreme discrimination, or IRL personal attacks will result in an instant ban.
  • Civility OOC: IC insults are part of RP, but out-of-character hostility/toxicity towards players or staff is not tolerated.

Gameplay & Economy

  • No Exploiting: Abusing bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics is a permanent ban offense. Report it.
  • No Scripting/Macros: Third-party software (AFK farming, key macros, aimbots) is strictly forbidden.
  • No Real-Money Trading (RMT): Trading IC cash or assets for IRL currency is prohibited.
  • Economic Realism: Don't hand out millions of dollars without valid IC reasons/contracts.

Vehicles & Property

  • Realistic Parking: Park in designated areas. Leaving cars in the middle of roads or hospital entrances will result in towing/fines.
  • Cop Baiting: Deliberately breaking traffic laws just to initiate a high-speed chase is fail RP.
  • Safe Zones (SZ): Hospitals and exact Spawn locations are safe zones. Criminal activities originating here are nullified.

General Conduct

  • Respect Staff: Admin decisions in-game are final. Handle disputes via forum/Discord tickets, not /b chat.
  • No Advertising: Do not advertise other servers or websites on our platforms.
  • No Alts: Multi-accounting to bypass bans or farm assets is forbidden.
  • Corruption: High-level faction corruption (e.g. Police Chiefs selling guns) must be pre-approved by Staff.

Rules are subject to change. Monitor Discord #announcements for updates. Last modified: 2026.
