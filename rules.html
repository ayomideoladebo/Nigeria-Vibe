Official Server Rules

Welcome to Nigeria Vibe RP. To ensure a fair and immersive experience for everyone, all players are required to read and adhere to the following rules. Ignorance is not an excuse for breaking them.

Roleplay & Immersion

  • Stay In-Character: No mixing OOC/IC chat during active RP. Use designated OOC channels for non-RP talk.
  • No Powergaming: Do not force actions on others without giving them a chance to respond or react.
  • No Metagaming: Do not use information gained outside of roleplay (e.g., Discord, streams) to influence in-character actions.
  • No Fail RP: Act realistically. Drive, speak, and react as a real person would in the situation.
  • Value Your Life (Fear RP): Respect dangerous situations. Do not act recklessly when your character's life is threatened.
  • Realistic Names: Character names must be realistic. No celebrity names, meme names, or random letters.

Crime & Violence

  • No RDM (Random Deathmatch): Killing another player must be preceded by high-quality roleplay and a valid reason.
  • No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch): Do not use your vehicle as a weapon to ram or kill others outside of a valid and escalated RP scenario.
  • High-Tier Crime: Robberies, kidnappings, or hits require significant planning, dialogue, and a valid reason. Low-effort crime is forbidden.
  • Gang Initiation: Gang activity and formation must be approved by server management before being established in-character.

Communication

  • Primary Language: English is the primary language for all public communications, both IC and OOC.
  • No Harassment: Hate speech, racism, discrimination, or personal attacks on other players are strictly forbidden.
  • Keep it Civil: In-character insults are part of RP, but OOC toxicity will not be tolerated.

Gameplay & Economy

  • No Exploiting: Abusing bugs, glitches, or unintended game mechanics for personal gain is a bannable offense.
  • No Scripting/Macros: Using third-party software to automate actions (e.g., AFK farming, macros) is forbidden.
  • No Real-Money Trading (RMT): Trading in-game assets for real money or assets on other servers is strictly prohibited.
  • Economic RP: All significant money transfers and sales must have a legitimate roleplay reason.

Vehicles & Property

  • Realistic Parking: Park vehicles in legal or designated areas. Do not block roads, entrances, or spawn points.
  • Respect Ownership: Do not steal, damage, or destroy another player's vehicle or property without a strong roleplay justification.
  • Safe Zones: Certain areas (like hospitals or police stations) may be designated safe zones. Combat is prohibited in these areas.

Jobs & Factions

  • Follow Commands: Adhere to your job or faction's chain of command and roleplay duties.
  • No Impersonation: Do not impersonate members of official factions or use their equipment without authorization.
  • No Corruption (Unless Approved): Corrupt roleplay within factions must be pre-approved by server staff to ensure it fits the server's narrative.

General Conduct

  • Respect Staff: Always respect decisions made by admins and staff. Use the proper channels to appeal if you disagree.
  • No Advertising: Do not advertise other servers, websites, or communities in-game or on Discord.
  • No Alt Accounts: Using alternate accounts to bypass bans, gain an unfair advantage, or disrupt roleplay is forbidden.
  • Donations are Perks: Donations support the server and provide cosmetic perks, but do not grant immunity from the rules.

Rules are subject to change. Last updated: September 19, 2025.
