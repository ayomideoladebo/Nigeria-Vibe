Accepting Applications

WHITELIST APPLICATION

Take your time. Detailed, high-quality answers significantly increase your chances of approval.

1. Identity verified

2. Roleplay Theory

Explain how you approach playing a character in a multiplayer environment.

Provide a specific scenario demonstrating what it is and why it ruins RP.

3. Character Profile

Where is your character from? Why did they move to Horizon City? What are their goals (legal or illegal)? Minimum 100 words.

By submitting, you agree to abide by all Horizon RP rules.

Applications are reviewed within 24-48 hours. Announcements made in Discord.
